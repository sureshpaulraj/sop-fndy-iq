## 1. Purpose

This SOP governs the creation, review, approval, distribution, and retirement of all Standard Operating Procedures across RCCB facilities. It ensures document consistency, version control, and regulatory compliance.

```
SOP-{DEPT}-{NUMBER}: {Title}
```
Examples:
- SOP-WH-042: Warehouse Spill Response & Cleanup
- SOP-EHS-001: Emergency Evacuation Plan
- SOP-QA-010: Quality Inspection & Sampling Procedures

- Minor revision: Increment decimal (v3.0 → v3.1)
- Editorial/typo fix: No version change (note in revision history)

## 3. SOP Lifecycle

### 3.1 Creation
1. Department manager identifies need for new SOP
2. Submit SOP Request Form (ADM-SOP-REQ-100) to Document Control
3. Assign author and subject matter expert (SME)
4. Author drafts SOP using RCCB SOP Template (ADM-TPL-001)
5. SME reviews for technical accuracy

### 3.2 Review & Approval
1. Department manager reviews and approves content
2. EHS reviews (if safety-related)
3. Legal/Compliance reviews (if regulatory)
4. Document Control reviews format and numbering
5. Final approval by department VP or designee

### 3.3 Distribution
1. Upload approved version to SharePoint SOP Library
2. Set metadata: department, effective date, review date, owner
3. Notify affected employees via email and training portal
4. Archive previous version (retain for 7 years)

### 3.4 Annual Review
1. Document Control sends 60-day review reminders to SOP owners
2. Owner reviews SOP with SME for accuracy
3. Submit "No Change" confirmation or revised version
4. Overdue reviews are escalated to department VP

### 3.5 Retirement
1. Submit SOP Retirement Form (ADM-SOP-RET-200)
2. Approval required from department VP and Compliance
3. Move to archived section in SharePoint
4. Retain archived SOPs for minimum 7 years
5. Update SOP index and cross-references
