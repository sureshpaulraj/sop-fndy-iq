# SOP-EHS-003: General Warehouse Safety Procedures

**Document ID:** SOP-EHS-003  
**Version:** 3.2  
**Effective Date:** January 15, 2026  
**Department:** Environmental Health & Safety  
**Owner:** Safety & Compliance Manager  
**Review Cycle:** Annual  
**Classification:** Internal — All Warehouse Employees  

---

## 1. Purpose

This SOP defines the general safety requirements for all personnel working in Contoso warehouse facilities. Compliance is mandatory and is a condition of employment.

## 2. Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)

### 2.1 Mandatory PPE — All Warehouse Areas
| PPE Item | Specification | When Required |
|----------|--------------|---------------|
| Hard hat | ANSI Z89.1 Type I | All dock and high-rack areas |
| Steel-toe boots | ASTM F2413 | At all times in warehouse |
| High-visibility vest | ANSI/ISEA 107 Class 2 | At all times in warehouse |
| Safety glasses | ANSI Z87.1 | At all times in warehouse |

### 2.2 Task-Specific PPE
| PPE Item | When Required |
|----------|---------------|
| Nitrile gloves | Handling product, chemicals, or cleaning |
| Chemical-resistant apron | Chemical mixing or spill cleanup |
| Face shield | Battery maintenance, chemical handling |
| Hearing protection | Noise levels > 85 dB (compressor rooms) |
| Cold-weather gear | Cold storage areas (≤ 35°F) |
| Fall protection harness | Work above 4 feet (order pickers, mezzanine) |

### 2.3 PPE Compliance
- PPE must be worn correctly at all times in designated areas
- Damaged PPE must be replaced immediately — report to supervisor
- PPE is provided at no cost to employees
- First violation: Verbal warning and retraining
- Second violation: Written warning
- Third violation: Suspension pending investigation

## 3. Housekeeping Standards

### 3.1 General Requirements
- Keep all aisles, exits, and fire lanes clear at all times
- Clean up spills immediately (see SOP-WH-042)
- Place wet floor signs for any wet surface
- Report burned-out lights within the current shift
- Dispose of waste in designated containers only

### 3.2 Pallet Stacking
| Area | Maximum Stack Height | Notes |
|------|---------------------|-------|
| Floor storage | 3 pallets high | Standard 48x40 pallets |
| Racking | Per rack design | Follow posted weight limits |
| Staging area | 2 pallets high | Temporary storage only |
| Dock area | 2 pallets high | Must not block egress |

### 3.3 Aisle Requirements
- Minimum aisle width: 12 feet (forklift travel lanes)
- Pedestrian walkways: 4 feet minimum, marked with yellow floor paint
- Fire lanes: Keep clear at all times — no exceptions
- Racking clearance: 18 inches minimum from sprinkler heads

## 4. Hazard Communication

### 4.1 Safety Data Sheets (SDS)
- SDS binder maintained at each department (physical copy)
- Electronic SDS available via Contoso Safety Portal
- All employees must know how to access SDS for chemicals they work with
- SDS must be reviewed before using any new chemical

### 4.2 Labeling Requirements
- All chemical containers must be labeled with:
  - Product name
  - Hazard pictogram(s)
  - Signal word (Danger or Warning)
  - Precautionary statements
- Secondary containers (spray bottles, etc.) must also be labeled
- Never remove or deface safety labels

## 5. Ergonomics and Manual Handling

### 5.1 Lifting Guidelines
- Maximum individual lift: **50 pounds**
- Use team lift (2+ people) for items > 50 pounds
- Use mechanical aids when available (pallet jacks, carts, lift tables)
- Proper lifting technique:
  1. Stand close to the load, feet shoulder-width apart
  2. Bend at the knees, not the waist
  3. Grip the load firmly with both hands
  4. Lift with your legs, keep back straight
  5. Keep the load close to your body
  6. Turn with your feet, not your torso

### 5.2 Repetitive Motion
- Rotate tasks every 2 hours when possible
- Take micro-breaks (30 seconds) every 30 minutes for repetitive tasks
- Report any discomfort or pain to supervisor immediately
- Ergonomic assessments available upon request

## 6. Reporting Requirements

### 6.1 Mandatory Reports
| Event | Report Within | Form |
|-------|--------------|------|
| Injury/illness | Immediately | WH-IR-300 |
| Near-miss | End of shift | EHS-NM-150 |
| Unsafe condition | Immediately | EHS-UC-175 |
| Equipment damage | Immediately | WH-EDR-200 |
| Spill | Immediately | EHS-IR-100 |

### 6.2 Reporting Channels
- **Supervisor** — first point of contact for all safety issues
- **Contoso Safety Hotline** — 1-800-Contoso-EHS (anonymous reporting available)
- **EHS Mobile App** — submit reports with photos from your device
- **Safety suggestion box** — located in break room

### 6.3 No Retaliation Policy
Contoso prohibits retaliation against any employee who reports a safety concern, near-miss, or violation in good faith. Reports may be made anonymously.
